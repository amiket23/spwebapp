About Us

Welcome to Vintage Grace, where we have been providing high-quality furniture for over a century. Established in 1347, our family-owned business has a long-standing reputation for exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Our mission is to bring you furniture that is not only beautiful and timeless but also built to last for generations. We believe that furniture should be an investment in your home, and we take great pride in offering a wide range of styles and designs that are carefully curated to fit any aesthetic.

At Vintage Grace, we understand that your home is a reflection of your personality and style, and our experienced team is here to help you create the perfect space. From classic to contemporary designs, our selection of furniture is handcrafted using only the finest materials and techniques.

We believe that the key to our success lies in our commitment to customer service. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff are dedicated to helping you find the perfect piece of furniture to fit your needs and budget. Whether you are furnishing a new home or simply looking to refresh your current space, we are here to help.

Thank you for choosing Vintage Grace, and we look forward to helping you create a beautiful and functional home that you will love for years to come.
