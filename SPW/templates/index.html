Our Story

Our story at Vintage Grace is one of passion, tradition, and timeless elegance. For over six centuries, we have been dedicated to the craft of furniture-making, using traditional techniques that have been passed down through generations. In 2003, we embarked on a new journey, bringing our exquisite collection of furniture online for customers all over the world to enjoy.

With Vintage Grace, you can own a piece of history that is both beautiful and functional. Our furniture is not just an investment, but a legacy to be cherished and passed down for generations to come. Join us on our journey and discover the timeless elegance of Vintage Grace furniture.
